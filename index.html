About DataHacks

DataHacks is UC San Diego’s premiere annual datathon, and the largest so far. Similar to a hackathon, you’ll be able to collaborate over multiple days to analyze and draw conclusions from provided datasets. Participants will be able to attend workshops to learn about the basics of data science, machine learning, and the libraries needed to succeed. This event will expose you to working with real world data, new friends you can pursue projects with after the event, and sponsors who are looking for avid data scientists to offer opportunities to. Join us now!
